# Install the App

The Metalify app is designed as a control panel that makes it easy to perform operations such as chart progression, placing orders, and making changes during MetaTrader4 (MT4) backtesting. This guide explains in detail the procedures from downloading the app to installation and initial setup.

## 1. Select "Download" from the Side Menu

1. Log in to the Metalify website and access the dashboard.
2. Click "Download" from the left side menu.

![AltText {priority}{768x432}](/img/download.en.png)

## 2. Download MetalifyController.exe

1. The download page provides installer files for different operating systems.
2. Windows users should click "MetalifyController.exe" to download.

> [!NOTE]
> We currently do not support MacOS, Linux, or other operating systems. We are considering future support for these platforms.

### Steps to bypass Windows Defender SmartScreen warning

When downloading, you may see a Windows Defender SmartScreen warning. Follow these steps to safely complete the download.

**Step 1: When the download warning appears, click the '...' button and select 'Keep'.**

![Windows Defender SmartScreen Step 1](/img/howto_download_controller1_en.png)

**Step 2: Click the '...' button again and click 'Keep anyway' to complete the download.**

![Windows Defender SmartScreen Step 2](/img/howto_download_controller2_en.png)

## 3. Install and Launch the App After Downloading

1. Open the downloaded installer file and follow the instructions to proceed with the installation.
2. Once installation is complete, click the Metalify app icon to launch it.

## 4. Enter Email Address and Password on the Authentication Screen When Launched

1. When you launch the app for the first time, an authentication screen will appear.
2. On this screen, enter your email address and password.
3. Once entered, click the "Login" button to log in.

![AltText {priority}{768x432}](/img/app_login.en.png)

> [!NOTE]
> Since June 2024, the authentication method has been changed to email address and password. If you were logged in using the previous authentication method (authentication by user ID), please log in with your account's email address and password.

## 5. Use During MetaTrader4 Backtesting

The Metalify app is used during MT4 backtesting. For specific operation methods and feature details, please refer to the [**User Guide**](/docs/control-panel).

---

This completes the installation and initial setup of the Metalify app. You now have a powerful tool to conduct MT4 backtests more efficiently and flexibly.
Next, you need to configure MetaTrader4 settings. Please see [**Configure MT4 Settings**](/docs/mt4-settings).
