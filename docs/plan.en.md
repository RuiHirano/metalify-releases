# Subscribe to a Plan

To make the most of Metalify, you need to subscribe to a plan. By subscribing to a plan, you can use Metalify's features. This guide explains in detail the procedure for subscribing to a plan from the Metalify website.

## 1. Log in from the Website

![AltText {priority}{768x432}](/img/home.en.png)

1. Access the [**official Metalify website**](https://metalify-fx.com).
2. Click the "Login" button in the upper right corner.
3. If you have already registered as a user, enter the email address and password you used during registration, then click the "Login" button to log in.
4. If you haven't registered yet, click the "Sign up" link displayed at the bottom to go to the user registration screen. Enter the required information and complete the user registration following the instructions. Then log in.

> [!NOTE]
> Since June 2024, the authentication method has been changed to email address and password. If you were logged in using the previous authentication method (authentication by user ID), please log in with your account's email address and password.

## 2. Select "Subscription" from the Side Menu

1. Navigate to the dashboard screen after logging in.
2. Click the "Subscription" item from the left side menu to access the plan management page.

![AltText {priority}{768x432}](/img/plan_no_subscription.en.png)

## 3. Enter Card Information

1. Click the "Enter Card Information" button for your desired plan in the plan application section.
2. Enter the required information in the displayed card information input fields.
3. Click the "Register Card" button at the bottom of the input fields.
4. When "Card information registered" is displayed, the card information entry is complete.

![AltText {priority}{768x432}](/img/plan_card_form.en.png)

> [!WARNING]
> If an error occurs when entering card information, check the entered information and try entering it again. If the error persists, please contact us from the [Contact](/support/contact) page.

> [!TIP]
> All credit card information is held by PAY.JP Inc., and Metalify does not hold any card information. You can make payments securely.

## 3. Subscribe to the Plan

1. After entering the card information, click the "Purchase Plan" button.
2. A confirmation screen will appear saying "You are about to purchase the plan. Are you sure?" Check the plan details and click the "OK" button if there are no issues.
3. "Plan purchased" will be displayed, and the subscribed plan will be added to your current plans.

![AltText {priority}{768x432}](/img/plan_complete_create_subscription.en.png)

---

This completes the plan subscription. After purchasing the plan, you can download the app and integrate it with MetaTrader4 to make the most of Metalify's features. Next, we'll explain in detail how to install the app. Please refer to [**Install the App**](/docs/install-app).
